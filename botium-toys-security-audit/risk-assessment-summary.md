# Risk Assessment Summary

## Audit Scope

The audit evaluated Botium Toys' entire security program, including:

- Internal network
- Employee devices
- Retail systems
- Inventory management systems
- Customer data storage
- Legacy systems

---

## Risk Score

Risk Score: 8/10 (High)

The organization currently lacks several critical security controls and does not fully comply with industry standards and regulations.

---

## Major Risks Identified

### Sensitive Data Exposure

All employees have access to internal data and potentially customer payment information.

Impact:
- Unauthorized access
- Insider threats
- Regulatory penalties

---

### Lack of Encryption

Customer payment information is stored without encryption.

Impact:
- PCI DSS non-compliance
- Increased breach severity

---

### Lack of Backups and Disaster Recovery

The organization has no disaster recovery plan and no backups of critical data.

Impact:
- Business interruption
- Permanent data loss

---

### Weak Access Control

Least privilege and separation of duties have not been implemented.

Impact:
- Unauthorized data access
- Fraud risk

---

### Inadequate Monitoring

No intrusion detection system is currently deployed.

Impact:
- Delayed threat detection
- Increased attacker dwell time
