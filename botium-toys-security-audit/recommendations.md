# Security Recommendations

## Critical Priority

### Implement Encryption

Protect customer payment information using encryption for data at rest and in transit.

Expected Benefit:
- PCI DSS compliance
- Reduced breach impact

---

### Implement Least Privilege

Restrict access based on job responsibilities.

Expected Benefit:
- Reduced insider threat risk
- Improved confidentiality

---

### Implement Separation of Duties

Divide sensitive responsibilities among multiple personnel.

Expected Benefit:
- Reduced fraud risk
- Improved accountability

---

## High Priority

### Develop Disaster Recovery Plan

Create documented recovery procedures for cyber incidents and system failures.

Expected Benefit:
- Improved business continuity

---

### Establish Regular Backups

Implement automated backups and test restoration procedures.

Expected Benefit:
- Faster recovery from incidents

---

### Deploy Intrusion Detection System

Monitor network activity for suspicious behavior.

Expected Benefit:
- Faster threat detection

---

## Moderate Priority

### Strengthen Password Policies

Require stronger passwords and enforce complexity standards.

---

### Implement Password Management System

Deploy centralized password management.

---

### Formalize Legacy System Maintenance

Create documented maintenance schedules and procedures.

---

### Improve Asset Management

Inventory and classify all assets according to sensitivity and business importance.
