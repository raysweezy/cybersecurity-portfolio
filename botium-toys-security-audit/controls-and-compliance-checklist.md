# Controls and Compliance Checklist

## Administrative Controls

### Least Privilege

Status: Not Implemented

All employees have broad access to internal data, increasing the risk of unauthorized access and data exposure.

### Disaster Recovery Plan

Status: Not Implemented

The organization does not have documented recovery procedures to restore operations after a disruption or disaster.

### Password Policy

Status: Partially Implemented

A password policy exists, but it does not meet current security standards and should be updated.

### Separation of Duties

Status: Not Implemented

Critical responsibilities are not separated among employees, increasing the risk of errors and fraud.

## Technical Controls

### Firewall

Status: Implemented

A properly configured firewall is in place to help protect the network from unauthorized access.

### Intrusion Detection System (IDS)

Status: Not Implemented

No intrusion detection system has been installed to monitor suspicious network activity.

### Backups

Status: Not Implemented

Critical business data is not regularly backed up, creating a significant risk of data loss.

### Antivirus

Status: Implemented

Antivirus software is installed and actively monitored to protect against malware.

### Encryption

Status: Not Implemented

Credit card and other sensitive data are stored without encryption, creating compliance and security risks.

### Password Management System

Status: Not Implemented

The organization does not use a centralized password management solution.

## Physical Controls

### Locks

Status: Implemented

Physical locks are installed to restrict unauthorized access to facilities and equipment.

### CCTV

Status: Implemented

Security cameras are operational and up to date.

### Fire Detection

Status: Implemented

Fire detection systems are functioning properly and help protect personnel and assets.

# Compliance Assessment

## PCI DSS Compliance

### Restricted Access to Cardholder Data

Status: Non-Compliant

Access controls for cardholder data are not adequately restricted.

### Secure Payment Processing Environment

Status: Non-Compliant

The payment processing environment does not meet PCI DSS security requirements.

### Data Encryption

Status: Non-Compliant

Sensitive payment card data is not encrypted.

### Secure Password Management

Status: Non-Compliant

Password management practices do not meet PCI DSS standards.

## GDPR Compliance

### Data Privacy Protection

Status: Compliant

Appropriate measures are in place to protect personal data.

### 72-Hour Breach Notification

Status: Compliant

Procedures exist to support breach notification requirements within the required timeframe.

### Data Inventory and Classification

Status: Compliant

Data assets have been identified and classified appropriately.

### Privacy Policies Enforced

Status: Compliant

Privacy policies are established and enforced across the organization.

## SOC Compliance

### User Access Policies

Status: Non-Compliant

User access controls and policies require improvement.

### Confidentiality of Sensitive Data

Status: Non-Compliant

Sensitive information is not adequately protected from unauthorized disclosure.

### Data Integrity

Status: Compliant

Controls are in place to maintain the accuracy and reliability of data.

### Data Availability

Status: Compliant

Systems and data remain available to support business operations.
